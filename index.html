Name your own price

Every night, you awaken in your studio and paint your masterpiece.

Or, at least, you try to.

Oh, if only your muse would return.

If only lifting your brush didn't fill your fingertips with agony.

Note: Web version may be laggy. For the best experience, please download and play on your OS!

This is a short visual novel about art, love and pain. It features:

  • 3.9k words
  • 3 endings
  • Hand-drawn artwork
  • Original music
  • Partial voice acting

Originally created in one week for Scream Jam 2023.

Content warnings: Violence, death, body horror, chronic illness, grief, dysfunctional relationships.
